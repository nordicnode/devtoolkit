About DevToolKit

Free, privacy-first developer tools that run entirely in your browser.

Our Mission

DevToolKit provides fast, reliable, and private developer utilities that work without signing up, without tracking, and without sending your data to any server. Every tool runs 100% client-side using JavaScript and modern browser APIs.

Why This Exists

Most online developer tools are bloated with ads, require accounts, or quietly log your data. We believe your code, your data, and your workflow should stay on your machine. DevToolKit was built to be the tool site we wished existed — one that respects your privacy and just works.

How We're Supported

DevToolKit is supported by unobtrusive advertising and relevant affiliate links to developer services we genuinely recommend. We never charge for features, and we never sell your data. If you find our tools useful, consider using our affiliate links when signing up for services — it helps keep the lights on at no extra cost to you.

Recommended Services

These are services we personally use and recommend:

  • Web Hosting: Looking for reliable hosting? Bluehost and HostGator offer solid shared hosting with 24/7 support.
  • Cloud Computing: AWS and DigitalOcean for scalable infrastructure.
  • Developer Education: Google online courses and Coursera for structured learning paths.

Technology

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no build steps, no npm dependencies. Hosted on GitHub Pages for zero-cost, high-availability delivery. The source is clean, fast-loading, and works offline after first visit.
