Privacy Policy

Your privacy is not negotiable. Here's exactly what we do and don't do.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your text, code, files, and queries never leave your device.

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as cookie-free, IP-masked services) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected. You will not be tracked across sites.

Cookies

We do not set first-party cookies. Third-party ad networks may set cookies for ad personalization. Essential functionality of the tools does not require any cookies.
